TRANSPORT



There are various modes of transportation in dubai

  • Bus: RTA buses are available at every RTA stop in dubai and connect dubai within itself and with other emirates.
  • Metro: RTA metro consists of three lines that help comute throughout dubai.
  • Taxi: RTA taxi are available any where in dubai and can get you anywhere in dubai fast and easy.
  • Tram: RTA tram helps you comute within the jumeirah area.
  • Water Taxi: RTA water taxi is only available to comute across Dubai creek.


IMPORTANT: all these transportation can be accessed by anyone possessing a valid NOL card
